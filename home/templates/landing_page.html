About CreatAR
A platform to create partnerships between brands and AR creatars. CreatAR is on the mission to make AR led advertising / consumer engagement campaigns mainstream.

CreatAR would celebrate creatars, their work, their passion for creating AR experiences for the brands that they work with. CreatAR would also inspire these creatars by showcasing AR experiences by other brands and fellow AR creatars from across the globe.
